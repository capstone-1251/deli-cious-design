About Us

Patricia Street Deli is a family-owned sandwich shop with deep roots in the Rockies. For over 20 years, we served the Jasper community, until the July 2024 wildfires took our home. Thanks to the generosity of our loyal customers and Kickstarter supporters, we’re now rebuilding in Edmonton.
